title: Deciding on Jekyll over Medium
---

**Reasons for Jekyll**
- Use Git
- Customize and integrate different content types
  - Blog posts
  - Project reflections
  - List of things
- Have my own space to tinker
- Appreciate the process of building the blog itself

**Reasons for Medium**
- Less work to make it look good
- A distribution network
- No commitment beyond the individual piece
